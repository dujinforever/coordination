The every datafolder includes ten datafile which are twicely measured in five different motion modes (walking,upstair,downstair,upslope,downslope).
Step_code1.py includes general function.
Coordination.py is a module with a class.
Coordination.joint_table is the cycle data;
Coordination.hip_knee is the coordiation pattern and variability between the trunk and shank;
Coordination.knee_ankle is the coordiation pattern and variability between the shank and foot;
The running example:
	runfile('.//Code//Coordination.py')
	runfile('.//Code//runfile.py')
